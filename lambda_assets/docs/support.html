Our Support Policy

We are always happy to help anyone who is experiencing problems with our themes. We have a dedicated support team ready to help you with any problems you may encounter.

To get in touch with our support team you need to visit our support forum. Support is limited to questions regarding the themeâ€™s features or problems with the theme. We do not support general Wordpress problems or 3rd party plugins you have installed. Also, it is important that you have read the documentation for the item purchased from our Documentation Library.

We will always respond to tickets as soon as we can. Our office working hours are 9:00 - 17:00 EET Monday - Friday

What is Customization

Customization is trying to achieve something that is not covered by the theme's options, therefore falls outside of the scope of our support service. We would suggest hiring a freelance developer and we would gladly help him with any questions he might have about Lambda.

Getting Support

Before creating a new ticket, please make sure that you have read our Documentation and watched our Video Tutorials. Also, search our forum for similar topics. Please always make sure that your topic has not already been answered in these areas.

If you need to ask for our help, feel free to do so by visiting our support forum.

Create a support account

On the left side of the support home page, there is a form that you need to fill, in order to become a registered member of our support forum.

Step 1 - Click on the Register ...button for the register form to appear.

Step 2 - Now that the proper form has appeared, fill in the First Name, Last Name, Email Addressand Choose a Password...fields.

Step 3 - Click on the Registerbutton.

Create a support ticket

After you have registered, you should be able to login from Ticksy Homepage, by filling in your Email Addressand Password. Once logged in, on the left side of the screen you should see a new form, which is the one you need to fill, in order to submit a new ticket.

Step 1 - Decide on whether you want to post a Public(visible to all users) or Private(visible only to you and Oxygenna team) ticket.

Step 2 - Choose the theme that your ticket is related to.

Step 3 - A new field will open up, requiring for a Purchase Code. See this section for more info on how to get your Purchase Code.

Step 4 - Fill in your Subjectand URL(optional).

Step 5 - Type the description of your problem, being as clear and precise as possible, so you can help us understand your problem and offer you our help without further ado.

Step 6 - Add any attachments that might help you describe your problem.

Step 7 - Click on Submit Ticketbutton.

Purchase Code

You can find your purchase code from Themeforeston the Downloads Pageby clicking the "Download"button next to the item, and then click the "Licence certificate & purchase code"link.
